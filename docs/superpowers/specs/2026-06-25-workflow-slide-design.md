# Design Prompt: Workflow Slide — Avalyse

**Data:** 2026-06-25
**Contexto:** Um único slide de workflow para usar durante a sales call. Mostrado somente quando o prospect pergunta "como funciona?". Deve ser legível em 90 segundos, um nó por vez. Não é uma apresentação — é um mapa visual de conversa.

---

## Prompt para Claude Design

Design a single dark-mode workflow slide for a Brazilian SaaS product called **Avalyse** — a done-for-you Google review automation service for local businesses.

### Purpose
This slide is shown during a 1:1 sales call when the prospect asks "how does it work?" The founder walks through each node in ~15 seconds. It must be instantly readable, minimal, and feel like a conversation tool — not a corporate deck.

### Canvas
- **Dimensions:** 1440×810px (16:9)
- **Background:** Deep navy `#0F1929` (dark mode)
- **Font:** Inter or similar geometric sans-serif
- **Brand accent:** Orange `#F97316`
- **Secondary text:** `#94A3B8` (muted slate)
- **Card background:** `#1E2D42` (slightly lighter than background)

### Layout
Horizontal linear flow — 4 nodes connected by right-facing arrows (`→`). Centered vertically on canvas. No title. No slide number. No logo in the main area (small wordmark bottom-right only).

Node width: ~280px each. Arrow gap: 40px. Total flow centered horizontally.

### The 4 Nodes (in order)

---

**Node 1 — CONEXÃO**

Icon reference: A dark electrical plug / webhook symbol. In the existing slides, Opção 02 uses a dark plug icon on a warm beige square. Recreate a similar plug or webhook icon in dark orange `#F97316` on a dark card.

- **Label (small caps, muted):** `CONEXÃO`
- **Title (white, 16px bold):** `Integração via API`
- **Outcome line (muted, 13px):** `Seu sistema de gestão avisa a Avalyse quando um atendimento termina`

---

**Node 2 — SOLICITAÇÃO**

Icon reference: WhatsApp logo or chat bubble. In the existing slides, Etapa 01 shows a phone mockup with a WhatsApp conversation. Here, use a simplified WhatsApp icon (white on green `#25D366`, or the logo) inside the card — no full phone mockup, just the icon.

- **Label (small caps, muted):** `SOLICITAÇÃO`
- **Title (white, 16px bold):** `WhatsApp automático`
- **Outcome line (muted, 13px):** `30 minutos após o atendimento — sem ninguém precisar lembrar`

---

**Node 3 — RESPOSTA**

Icon reference: Google Maps "G" pin or five stars. In the existing slides, Etapa 03 shows a browser mockup with a Google review. Here, use a simplified Google "G" icon or a ★★★★★ line in yellow `#FBBF24` inside the card.

- **Label (small caps, muted):** `RESPOSTA`
- **Title (white, 16px bold):** `Avaliação respondida`
- **Outcome line (muted, 13px):** `Google vê que você é ativo — ranking no Maps sobe`

---

**Node 4 — PROVA SOCIAL**

Icon reference: Instagram camera icon or Stories ring. In the existing slides, Etapa 04 shows a phone with an Instagram Story. Here, use a simplified Instagram camera icon with a gradient border (pink→orange, Instagram-style) inside the card.

- **Label (small caps, muted):** `PROVA SOCIAL`
- **Title (white, 16px bold):** `Story automático`
- **Outcome line (muted, 13px):** `Avaliação 5⭐ vira conteúdo no seu Instagram — sem abrir o app`

---

### Arrows
- Color: `#F97316` (orange)
- Style: thin solid arrow `→`, weight 1.5px
- Centered vertically between nodes

### Cards
- Border radius: 12px
- Subtle border: `1px solid #2A3F5C`
- Icon area: 40×40px rounded square, top-center of card, with brand-colored icon
- Padding: 20px
- No shadow — flat dark design

### Typography hierarchy
1. Label (node type): `10px`, `letter-spacing: 0.15em`, `#64748B`, ALL CAPS
2. Title: `15px`, `600 weight`, `#FFFFFF`
3. Outcome: `12px`, `400 weight`, `#94A3B8`, max 2 lines

### What to avoid
- No slide titles or section headers
- No bullet lists
- No decorative gradients on background
- No corporate/startup clichés (no rocket emojis, no "scale" language)
- No animation specs (static only)
- No pricing or CTA — this is a conversation tool, not a landing page
